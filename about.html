Who We Are

NEXUS Youth Initiative for Social Transformations is a youth-led, non-profit organisation based in Abuja, Nigeria. We exist to inspire, empower, and mobilise young people as catalysts for positive social change.

Through education, advocacy, and community engagement, we build sustainable frameworks for civic participation. We believe that an empowered youth demographic is the cornerstone of a transparent, accountable, and thriving society.

↗

Our Mission

To inspire, empower, and mobilise young people as catalysts for positive social transformation. We strive to provide the tools, platforms, and knowledge necessary for youth to actively participate in shaping their communities and nation.

◎

Our Vision

To build a society where empowered and socially conscious young people lead transformative change, creating inclusive, equitable, and progressive communities across Nigeria and beyond.
